Signing in to RaidOps is done exclusively through Discord — no separate account or password to create.

### Why Discord?

Discord is the backbone of RaidOps: it's your identity, the list of guilds you have access to, and your permissions within each of them.

- **Your Discord account is your RaidOps account.** Nothing extra to create or remember.
- **RaidOps guilds are backed by a Discord server.** If you're a member of the server, RaidOps knows automatically.
- **Your permissions come from your Discord roles** on the guild's server. No one needs to grant them manually in RaidOps — everything follows what's already configured on Discord.

> Your Discord roles are checked again on every sign-in, never cached for long. If your role changes on Discord, it's reflected in RaidOps as soon as you sign in again.

### Signing in

Click the account icon in the top right, then **Login**. You're redirected to Discord to authorize access, then sent back to RaidOps automatically once signed in.

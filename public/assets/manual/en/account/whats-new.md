The 📣 icon in the header takes you to **What's new** — the list of features added to RaidOps over time.

<img src="/assets/manual/en/account/whats-new-icon.png" alt="What's new icon with badge in the header" width="72" />

A badge appears on the icon when there's something you haven't seen yet. It disappears as soon as you open the page — no need to read entries one by one.

Every entry marked **New** links to the matching manual guide, if you want to dig deeper into that feature.

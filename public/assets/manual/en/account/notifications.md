The 🔔 bell at the top of the app flags actions you need to take — typically after a new feature ships that requires a setting on your end.

<img src="/assets/manual/en/account/notification-bell.png" alt="Bell with badge in the header" width="72" />

### Today

The only active case for now: if you're an administrator of a guild whose **Officer access threshold** hasn't been configured yet, a notification reminds you with a direct link to that guild's settings.

![Notification dropdown open](/assets/manual/en/account/notification-dropdown.png)

### Never show again

Each notification has a **Configure** button (jumps straight to the flagged setting) and **Never show again**.

> Think before clicking **Never show again**: unlike a simple dismiss, this choice is permanent for this guild. If the underlying issue isn't actually fixed, the notification won't come back to remind you.

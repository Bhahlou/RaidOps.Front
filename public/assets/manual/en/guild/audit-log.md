> Officer only.

The **Audit log** lists the history of notable actions performed on the guild: setting changes, members added/removed/kicked, rank changes, and more.

![Audit log overview](/assets/manual/en/guild/audit-log-overview.png)

### Columns

Each entry shows the **Actor** behind the action, its **Category** (Guild, Settings, Roster), the **Action** taken, a **Before / After** summary of the change, and the **Date**.

### Filter and sort

Every filterable column has a funnel icon with a search field — handy to quickly find every action by a specific member, or every change in a given category. A date range picker (**From** / **To**) lets you narrow down the period. Click a column header to sort.

### Load more

The list is paginated — click **Load more** at the bottom to see older entries.

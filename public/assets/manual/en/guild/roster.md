The **Roster** page lists every member of the guild and lets you manage your own characters in this guild.

![Roster page overview](/assets/manual/en/guild/roster-overview.png)

### Search and filter

Use the search bar to find a player or character by name. The filter icons let you narrow down by class, main spec, rank (**Main**, **Split**, **Alt**), or join date range. The result count shows at the top of the list, and **Clear filters** resets everything at once.

### Sort

Click a column header (Player, Character, Class, Level, Rank, Joined) to sort the list by that criterion.

### Change a character's rank

The rank (Main/Split/Alt) is editable directly in the table if you're an **Officer**, or if it's your own character. For anyone else, the rank shows read-only.

### Kick a member

> Officer only.

Each row's action menu (⋮) offers **View character**, and for Officers, **Kick from guild**. A confirmation is required before kicking — the character can be added back later, and its data stays intact.

![Row action menu](/assets/manual/en/guild/roster-row-menu.png)

<img src="/assets/manual/en/guild/roster-kick-confirm.png" alt="Kick confirmation" width="420" />

### Manage your own characters in this guild

The **My characters in this guild** panel on the right only affects your own characters — no Officer restriction here.

- **Add a character** opens the list of your characters not yet in this guild, each with a rank to pick and a **Join** button.
- For a character already in the guild, you can change its rank directly, or click **Leave guild**.

This page registers a Discord server on RaidOps. You reach it by clicking **Register** or **Complete** from [My guilds](/manual/guild/guild-list), or automatically if it's the only server you have left to register.

![Register a guild — step 1](/assets/manual/en/guild/register-invite.png)

### 1. Invite bot

Click **Invite the bot and register** — you're redirected to Discord to authorize the RaidOps bot to join your server. Once the invitation is accepted, this step is marked complete automatically and you move to the next one.

### 2. Settings

This step is exactly the form described in [Guild settings](/manual/guild/settings) (timezone, roster access, Officer access threshold). Once saved, you're redirected to the guild's dashboard.

> You need to be an administrator of the Discord server to access this page. If the guild is already registered and configured, RaidOps redirects you straight to its dashboard.

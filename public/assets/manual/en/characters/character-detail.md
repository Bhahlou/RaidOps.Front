The character sheet gathers everything about one of your imported characters: gear, BIS list, raid specs, and the guilds it's joined.

![Character sheet overview](/assets/manual/en/characters/character-detail-overview.png)

### Available actions

On your own character sheet, three actions are available in the top right:

![Action buttons on the character sheet](/assets/manual/en/characters/character-detail-actions.png)

- **Resync from Battle.net** — triggers a resync with Battle.net. Right now, this mostly refreshes the character's avatar; gear syncing will come with the Gear feature (see below).
- **Configure raid-viable specs** — changes which specs this character can play in raid, and its main spec.
- **Deactivate from RaidOps** — removes this character from RaidOps. This doesn't delete its data: if you import it again later, its history is recovered.

### What's on the sheet

- **Gear** and **BIS list** — cards reserved for two upcoming features, not active yet.
- **Guilds** — on your own sheet only, the list of RaidOps guilds this character has joined, with its rank in each.

> You only see the management actions (resync, specs, deactivation) and the Guilds section on your own characters. Another player's character sheet is read-only.

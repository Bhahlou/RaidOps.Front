The first time you land on RaidOps, a 4-step wizard guides you automatically — you can't get lost, and you can leave and come back anytime, it picks up exactly where you left off.

![Wizard overview](/assets/manual/en/welcome/wizard-step1.png)

### How it makes things easier

- **One step at a time.** The wizard only shows what's left to do — no need to guess what comes next.
- **Nothing gets lost along the way.** Signing in with Discord or linking Battle.net briefly takes you away from RaidOps (redirect to their site) — the wizard remembers and brings you right back where you were.
- **Each step checks itself off automatically** as soon as it's done. Nothing to confirm yourself, except the guild step (see below).
- If your account is already fully set up, you won't even see this screen — straight to your guild list instead.

### 1. Discord

Sign in with your Discord account — see [why Discord](/manual/account/discord-auth) is the first step of everything.

### 2. Guild

This step adapts to your situation: a guild you already have access to, and/or Discord servers you can register yourself if you're an admin.

![Guild step of the wizard](/assets/manual/en/welcome/wizard-step2.png)

- Already have a guild registered on RaidOps? It shows up automatically, labeled **Roster accessible** or **Limited access** depending on your role.
- Admin of a Discord server not registered yet? A **Register** button takes you to invite the bot.
- Bot already invited but settings not saved yet? The [guild settings](/manual/guild/settings) form appears right here — no need to go elsewhere.
- No guild available? Join a Discord server already registered on RaidOps, or become an administrator of one to register it yourself.

Click **Continue** to move to the next step — whether or not this one is done, you can always register or join a guild later.

### 3. Battle.net

Picks up exactly the flow detailed in [Import your character](/manual/getting-started/create-character): link your account, sync, then pick which of your synced characters (grouped by realm) to import.

![Battle.net step of the wizard](/assets/manual/en/welcome/wizard-step3.png)

### 4. Roster

Every guild you have access to shows up as a card with your eligible characters. Check the ones you want to sign up — a dropdown then appears to pick their rank (**Main**, **Split**, or **Alt**, pre-filled as Main), which determines their status in that guild's roster. Click **Join** to confirm.

![Roster step of the wizard](/assets/manual/en/welcome/wizard-step4.png)

The **Finish** button at the bottom stays disabled until at least one character has joined a guild. If no guild is eligible at all, a message shows up instead with a **Skip this step** button to finish right away without joining anything.
